Questions

Helpful details before booking

How early should I book?

For bridal hand henna, 1 to 3 months in advance is ideal. Smaller party and festival designs can often be booked closer to the date.

Do you travel to the venue?

Yes. Travel can be arranged for homes, hotels, halls, and destination events. Travel fees depend on location and timing.

Can you work with sensitive skin?

Yes. Share allergies or sensitivities during consultation so the henna session can be planned carefully.

How long does bridal henna take?

Most full bridal hand henna sessions take 2 to 5 hours depending on detail level, coverage, and custom elements.
